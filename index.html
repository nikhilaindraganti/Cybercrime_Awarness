Cybercrime Awareness

What is Cybercrime?

Cybercrime refers to criminal activities carried out using computers and the Internet. It includes hacking, phishing, identity theft, and more.

Types of Cybercrime

  • Phishing scams
  • Ransomware attacks
  • Online fraud
  • Identity theft
  • Malware infections

Protecting Yourself Online

Follow these tips to stay safe online:

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Avoid clicking on suspicious links or attachments
  • Keep your software and antivirus updated
  • Be cautious with sharing personal information
